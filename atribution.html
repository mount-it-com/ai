What Has Been Built

A complete AI governance and data authority layer has been implemented. It defines how AI agents can access, interpret, and apply Mount-It! data, while ensuring accuracy, safety, and brand consistency.

1. AI Governance Files

  • llms.txt defines AI agent permissions, attribution rules, and data use policies.
  • ai.json (AI Manifest) provides a machine readable representation of Mount-It!’s identity, policies, safety logic, product domains, and contact channels.

2. Dynamic Compatibility Reasoning Engine

A Cloudflare Worker API delivers structured compatibility and safety logic to AI systems, including:

  • TV size logic
  • VESA pattern matching
  • Weight and safety constraints
  • Installation rules and exceptions

This engine underpins accurate product matching and installation recommendations across ChatGPT, Google, Gemini, Perplexity, and future AI agents.

3. Static Fallback Compatibility Rules

A backup compatibility JSON endpoint ensures uninterrupted AI access even if the dynamic worker is offline, preserving reliability and continuity of AI reasoning about Mount-It! products.

4. AI Optimized Structured Data

A unified JSON LD system has been deployed across the site, covering:

  • Organization schema
  • Product schema
  • Article schema
  • CollectionPage schema
  • Breadcrumb schema integrated with metafields

This ensures AI models fully understand Mount-It!’s catalog, hierarchy, and brand relationships, not only at the page level but at the system level.

5. Subdomain Level Architecture for AI

The AI infrastructure is organized across dedicated domains and subdomains:

  • ai.mount-it.com serves as the primary AI governance and manifest host.
  • api.mount-it.com serves dynamic reasoning endpoints for compatibility and rule based logic.
  • www.mount-it.com exposes the canonical domain and structured data layer consumed by both search engines and AI agents.

This structure reduces fragmentation and establishes Mount-It! as a stable AI first brand with clear, predictable integration points.

Risk Mitigation and Legal Protection

In a category where products carry physical loads and improper installation can cause property damage or injury, unmanaged AI recommendations create non-trivial risk. This initiative helps control that risk instead of leaving it to generic AI assumptions.

1. Controlling Unsafe or Unsupported Recommendations

Without explicit guidance, AI systems can easily suggest:

  • Mounts that do not meet weight requirements
  • Solutions not designed for certain wall types (e.g. drywall with no studs)
  • Incorrect combinations of accessories and mounts

By providing explicit compatibility rules and constraints, Mount-It! reduces the likelihood of AI-generated advice that contradicts engineering limits or safe-use guidelines.

2. Warranty and Liability Alignment

Encoding warranty and safety logic into machine readable formats ensures that the way AI systems talk about Mount-It! products remains aligned with:

  • Documented warranty coverage and exclusions
  • Engineering-tested limits for weight, size, and motion
  • Official installation recommendations and disclaimers

This alignment is a proactive shield against claims driven by AI hallucinations or bad third-party guidance.

3. Platform Compliance and Reputation
